FAQ

Questions answered.

Still on the fence? Here are the most common things people ask before they sign up.

1 Do I need any technical skills to set this up?
Zero. We handle 100% of the technical side — setup, training, and integration. You just tell us about your business and we build it. Most clients are live within 48 hours without touching a single line of code.
2 What channels does the AI work on?
Currently WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, SMS (via Twilio), and Telegram. Website chat widget is also available on the Standard and Pro plans. We're adding more channels monthly.
3 What happens when the AI doesn't know the answer?
It gracefully tells the customer it'll get them connected with a human, logs the conversation, and alerts you immediately. You never leave a customer hanging — and we continuously improve the AI's knowledge base.
4 Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No contracts. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in. Most clients stay because the ROI is obvious within the first 30 days.
5 How does billing work and what's included in setup?
Monthly billing via credit card or PayPal. Setup is included in your first month — no extra fees. That includes AI training, channel connections, custom scripts, and a 30-min onboarding call. After that, your monthly fee covers everything: hosting, maintenance, updates, and support.
